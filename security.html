Security at LockHabit

Our approach to keeping your information and funds safe.

Bank-grade custody

LockHabit is not a bank. Deposit custody is provided by regulated partner banks. When deposits are held at partner banks and structured for pass-through coverage, funds are eligible for FDIC insurance up to legal limits per depositor, per bank.

Never lent out

Your funds are reserved for you and are not lent to third parties. Emergency access is a secured self-loan to you, backed by your locked balance.

Data protection

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access controls and least-privilege
  • Audit logging and monitoring

Responsible disclosure

Report security issues to security@lockhabit.com. If you use PGP, our key and policy live at /.well-known/security.txt.

Hard-lock rules (pre-launch summary)

  • Funds remain locked until the unlock date.
  • Emergency withdrawals require approval.
  • Non-emergency early break attempts may incur a 5% fee.
