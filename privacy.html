Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and use your data only to operate and improve LockHabit.

Last updated:

What we collect

  • Contact info (e.g., email) when you join the waitlist or contact us.
  • Usage data (pages, clicks) via privacy-friendly analytics to improve the site.
  • Account data (when available): identity verification, transactions—handled under financial regulations and partner-bank agreements.

How we use data

  • Operate, maintain, and improve LockHabit.
  • Communicate about launches, updates, and support.
  • Meet legal, regulatory, and security obligations.

Sharing

  • Vendors who help us run LockHabit (cloud, analytics, email) under agreements.
  • Compliance with law, regulation, or to protect users and our service.
  • No selling of personal data.

Cookies & analytics

We aim to use privacy-first analytics (e.g., Plausible) without cross-site tracking. You can block cookies in your browser if you prefer; parts of the site may rely on cookies for sign-in when available.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, or as required by law.

Your choices

  • Opt out of non-essential emails anytime.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion (subject to legal limits).

Security

We use industry-standard protections. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify you of material incidents as required by law.

Children

LockHabit is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we collected such data, we’ll delete it.

Changes

We may update this Policy. We’ll revise the date and, for material changes, provide notice.
