LockHabit Privacy Notice

Effective:

We respect your privacy and only collect data needed to operate the waitlist and improve the site. We do not sell your personal information.

What we collect

  • Waitlist data: email, optional name and preferred term.
  • Usage data: device/browser info, pages viewed, and events (via privacy-first analytics like Plausible).

How we use data

  • To manage the waitlist and send early-access updates.
  • To secure, maintain, and improve the site.
  • To comply with law and prevent abuse.

Sharing

We may share data with service providers who assist us (hosting, analytics, email) under contracts that require confidentiality and appropriate safeguards. We may disclose information if required by law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method is 100% secure, but we aim to protect your information and restrict access to authorized personnel and vendors.

Your choices

  • You can unsubscribe from emails at any time via the link in a message.
  • Contact us to access or delete your waitlist data.

Children

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this Notice. Material changes will be posted here with a new date.
