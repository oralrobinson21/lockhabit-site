LockHabit Terms

Hard Lock. Deposits are locked until the selected unlock date. Early withdrawal is not permitted except for approved emergencies. Non-emergency early break attempts incur a 5% fee.

Flat Unlock Fees. At unlock, a flat fee applies based on term: $3 (1–3 months), $2 (4–6 months), $1 (6–12+ months).

Yield. Balances earn a transparent APR (e.g., 3% APR) prorated by time in lock. Rates may change.

Custody & FDIC. Banking/custody provided by regulated partner banks. When structured for pass-through coverage, deposits are eligible for FDIC insurance up to applicable limits per depositor, per bank. Coverage does not apply to non-deposit products.

Emergency Self-Loans. If eligible, you may borrow against your balance. Terms, eligibility checks, and reporting may apply; on-time payments may help build credit.

We are not a bank. LockHabit is a financial technology company; banking services by partner banks. Program details and disclosures provided prior to launch.
