LockHabit Terms

Hard Lock. Funds remain locked until the chosen unlock date. Early withdrawal is not permitted except for approved emergencies. Non-emergency early break attempts incur a 5% fee.

Flat Unlock Fees. A flat fee applies at unlock: $3 (1–3 months), $2 (4–6 months), $1 (6–12+ months).

Yield. Balances earn transparent 3% APR, prorated by lock duration.

Emergency Self-Loans. If eligible, you may borrow against your locked balance with 5% interest. 100% of this interest is credited back to your original lock. Loans are reported to major credit bureaus; on-time payments may help build credit.

Custody & FDIC. Banking services and deposit custody are provided by regulated partner banks. When structured for pass-through coverage, funds are eligible for FDIC insurance up to applicable limits per depositor, per bank.

We are not a bank. LockHabit is a financial technology company. Banking services by partner banks.
