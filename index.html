FAQ

How is interest calculated?

We compound weekly. Choose a term (3, 6, 12, or 24 months) and we apply the posted APR for that term to your balance as you deposit $200 weekly (or your custom amount).

What is “vested”?

Vested is your principal (all deposits) plus interest. It’s the amount used to calculate your emergency loan limit.

What is the emergency loan limit?

Up to 80% of your vested amount. Loans are self-backed and designed to keep you on track.

Is my money FDIC insured?

When deposits are held at partner banks and structured for pass-through coverage, balances are eligible for FDIC insurance up to applicable limits per depositor, per bank.
