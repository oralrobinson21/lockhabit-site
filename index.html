How LockHabit works

1) Choose your lock

Pick 3, 6, 12, or 24+ months. Set an auto-deposit you can live with (e.g., $200 bi-weekly). Funds move into your hidden vault.

2) Grow automatically

Balances grow at a fixed APR tier (prorated by time in lock). Your deposits are never lent out.

3) Unlock or borrow

At maturity, withdraw principal + interest (less posted fees), or extend your lock. Emergencies? Borrow against your own balance.

FAQ

What exactly is LockHabit?

A hard-lock savings and self-credit-builder system. We help you remove temptation, save on purpose, and build a safe emergency back-up using your own balance.

Is my money safe?

Your funds are not lent to other customers. We intend to custody deposits at regulated partner banks with pass-through FDIC insurance up to legal limits (per depositor, per bank).

Can I withdraw early?

No — it’s a hard lock. Early withdrawal isn’t allowed except for approved emergencies. Non-emergency break attempts incur posted fees.

Do I earn interest while locked?

Yes. Fixed APR tiers (e.g., 2.0%–3.5%) are prorated by time in lock. Rates for new locks may change with notice.

How does the emergency loan work?

Borrow up to 80% of your vested balance. It’s 0% for the first 4 months, then 6% APR. Intended to be credit-bureau-reported (on-time payments can help build credit; missed payments may harm credit).

What about the high-risk option?

It’s optional. Variable APR, can lose value (historically –10% to +20%). If enabled, there’s a $2/month fee and a 2% close-out fee. Not investment advice; returns not guaranteed.

Can I extend my term?

Yes. At the end of your term you can extend and keep compounding, or withdraw within the normal processing window.
