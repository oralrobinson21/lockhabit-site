FAQ

Is this a hard lock?

Yes. Funds remain locked until the chosen unlock date. Early withdrawal is not permitted except for approved emergencies. Non-emergency early break attempts incur a 5% fee.

Do I earn interest while locked?

Yes. Balances earn the disclosed APR, compounded weekly and prorated by time in lock. Rates may change with notice.

How does the emergency line work?

You can borrow up to 80% of your vested amount. The line is 0% for the first 4 months, then 6% APR. After payoff, interest is credited back to your balance. On-time payment may help your credit; late payment may harm it.

Is my money FDIC insured?

Deposits are held at regulated partner banks. When structured for pass-through coverage, balances are eligible for FDIC insurance up to applicable limits per depositor, per bank.

What about the High-Risk Growth option?

It is variable APR (7.8‚Äì20%) and you can lose value. It‚Äôs optional and includes extra disclosures before opt-in. Consider your risk tolerance.

What is the Standard Credit Builder?

You can access up to 65% of your vested balance at 6% APR. After payoff, the interest is credited back to your balance. We intend to report on-time payments to major bureaus.

How does the Emergency Credit Builder differ?

Emergency Credit Builder lets you access up to 80% of your vested amount with 0% for the first 2 months (no payment due), then 6% APR. Interest after the promo is credited back after payoff. Your hard lock remains intact.
